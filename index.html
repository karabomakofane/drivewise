5 Golden Driving Rules

The fundamental principles every driver must master.

01

Always Check Mirrors

Every 5–8 seconds and before every manoeuvre. Know what's around you at all times.

02

Follow the MSPSL Routine

Mirror, Signal, Position, Speed, Look — every time you change direction or merge.

03

Maintain Safe Following Distance

At least a 3-second gap. Double it in rain or poor visibility.

04

Obey All Road Signs

Regulatory signs are legally binding — ignoring them is a criminal offence.

05

Zero Distractions

A 2-second distraction at 60 km/h covers 33 metres completely blind.
